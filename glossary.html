Know the Lingo

Get comfortable with the characters language and slang!

For this page, I just want to have a list of the common slang words that are heard in the movies and shows. I'd like to create an alphabetical scrolling navigation bar for this page, so that users can browse terms by starting letter.

B

Bantha

A giant, hairy animal from the planet Tatooine. They have large spiraling horns like goats. They were often used by Tusken Raiders as mounts.

Bantha crap

Like bullcrap or being called full of crap, but speficially Bantha crap.

Bantha fodder

A big loser. Worthless. Something only a Bantha would eat.

Blast!

A swear of sorts, similar to "darn!"

Bombad

Either a rank in the Gungan army or a term to describe the importance of something.

Bucket

A stormtrooper helmet.

Buckethead

An insult directed at stormtroopers and the helmets they wear.

Bug

Slang for a Geonosian, generally used by clone troopers.

C

Chuba!

"You!" or "Hey you!" in Huttese.

Civvies

Civilian clothing.

Clanker

Clone trooper slang for battle droids.

D

Droid Poppers

Clone trooper slang for EMP grenades.

E

Exhaust port

A swear meaning someone's butt.

F

Flyboy

An especially talented pilot like Anakin Skywalker or Han Solo.

G

Gas-gulper

A term for the massive Neebray mantas.

Graysuit

Slang for an Imperial officer, usually used by stormtroopers.

H

Have bigger burra fish to fry

Having more important things to do.

I

Imp

Slang for Imperials.

In Malachor

A term similar to "in hell," like "there's no way in hell I'm doing this."

J

Jedi

An ancient order of force-users who harness the light-side of the force and follow principles focused on calmness, meditation, and controlling ones emotions. They were a sign of peace and justice in the galaxy for thousands of years.

Jedi starfighter

The unique starfighters generally used by the Jedi.

K

Karabast

A Lasat swear signifying frustration.

L

Laserbrain

An insult.

Lightsaber

An ancient weapon used by the Jedi, Sith, and other force-users. It is a laser sword powered by kyber crystals that takes intense skill and training.

M

Metalhead

Slang for a droid.

Moof-milker

A rude term for a dumb person.

More than one way to skin a womp rat

A phrase meaning there are multiple ways to achieve something.

N

Nerfherder

An insult famously used by Princess Leia.

P

Padawan

A young Jedi training under a Jedi Master or Jedi Knight. Padawans learn under their masters until they are ready to undergo trials to become Jedi Knights.

Poodoo

A huttese swear word meaning fodder.

Popper

Slang for a grenade, generally used by clone troopers.

R

Reg

A regular clone trooper. This term was used often by Clone Force 99, a group of clones with unique genetic mutations.

Rock

A derogatory term for a planet.

Rust bucket

A rude term for an old or battered droid.

S

Seppies

A slang term used for Separatists during the Clone Wars.

Sleemo

An insult in Huttese, meaning "slimeball."

Sith

An ancient order of force-users who harness the dark-side of the force and adhere to a set of principles focused on hate, anger, greed, and power. They're easily recognizable by their red lightsabers.

Spacer

Someone who spends much of their life in space.

Space baby

Someone born and raised in space.

Spice

A narcotic minded in the spice mines of Kessel. It is generally minded via slave labor, and often smuggled.

Sun bonnet

Slang for a clone trooper helmet.

U

Utinni

"Come here!" in the language used by Jawas. It is generally yelled.

V

Victory kid

A child born after the fall of the Galactic Empire.

W

Wild bantha chase

Similar to a wild goose chase.

Womp rat

Womp rats are pets from Tatooine that were often hunted for sport.

Y

Youngling

A youngling is the term for force-senstive children, usually those undergoing training at the Jedi temple.
