Welcome to Corscant

Star Wars is one of the most famous movie franchises of all time. Starting with the Original Trilogy in the 70s, the franchise has now grown into three trilogies, several animated and live-action television series, video games, books, toys, and more. As a lifelong Star Wars fan, I love talking to others about Star Wars and bringing new people into the community. With that in mind, I thought it would be useful for those unfamilar or new to the franchise to have a little guide. Hence, The Coruscant Gazette was born. I hope these pages can help you get into the amazing world of Star Wars.
